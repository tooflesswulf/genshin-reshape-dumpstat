01

Reshape guarantees

Each upgrade roll lands on one of the artifact's 4 substats at random. Reshaping lets you pick 2 substats, and the game promises at least 2 rolls will land on your picks. The game fulfills that promise by intervening only when the remaining rolls are your last chance to hit the promise, which has strong implications on the resulting statistics.

02

Consequence of reshape guarantees

The main consequence is that only outcomes with exactly 2 picked rolls are boosted. The below table illustrates the problem: high-value outcomes are unaffected by choosing (CRIT & ATK%) vs (CRIT & DEF). The starred outcome distinguishes the two choices, which is only boosted by reshaping on DEF.

Outcomes (top 5, sorted) No reshape CRIT & ATK% CRIT & DEF
4 CRIT 0.39% 0.39% 0.39%
3 CRIT + 1 ATK% 1.56% 1.56% 1.56%
2 CRIT + 2 ATK% ★ 2.34% 2.34% 4.30%boosted
3 CRIT + 1 junk 3.12% 3.12% 3.12%
1 CRIT + 3 ATK% 2.34% 2.34% 0%
CRIT Rate ATK% dump (HP / DEF) ★ = the best roll your pick can change  ·  5 of 35 possible outcomes shown

With a higher bar for accepting the reshape outcome, the boost to the (2 CRIT + 2 ATK%) bucket dominates the comparison between the two choices.
