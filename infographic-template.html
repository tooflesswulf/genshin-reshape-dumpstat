Genshin artifact reshape math

Artifact reshaping & dump stats.

Artifact reshaping lets you guarantee 2 substat rolls. The unintuitive part: if you only keep well-rolled pieces, spending one of those picks on a stat you don't want can yield better results.

A calculator is available at the Genshin Optimizer under the tab (Artifact Upgrader → Reshape). Requires the Irminsul scanner for artifact initial value data.

01

Reshape guarantees

Each upgrade roll lands on one of the artifact's 4 substats at random. Reshaping lets you pick 2 substats, and the game promises at least 2 rolls will land on your picks. The game fulfills that promise by landing the last few rolls on your picks if the first few go poorly, like a pity system. However, this also means that only outcomes with exactly 2 picked rolls are boosted, and the rest are unchanged.

02

Consequence of reshape guarantees

The main statistical consequence is that only outcomes with exactly 2 picked rolls are boosted. The table below shows the best 5 roll outcomes* and compares the roll counts for (CRIT & ATK%) vs (CRIT & DEF). There's no difference in the top 2 outcomes because the roll counts are above 2, but the starred (2 CRIT + 2 ATK%) outcome boosts (CRIT & DEF), tilting the scales its favor.
